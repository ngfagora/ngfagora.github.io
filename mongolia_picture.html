Milky Way at Northern Mongolia

In the remote wilderness of Northern Mongolia's Khövsgöl province, a breathtaking spectacle unfolds beneath the pristine night skies. Free from the glow of urban lights, the Milky Way paints a captivating picture with its intricate web of stars, dust, and nebulae, casting a spellbinding glow upon the darkness. Notably, a subtle greenish hue, emanating from self-illuminated atmospheric gases, adds an enchanting layer to the cosmic panorama.
